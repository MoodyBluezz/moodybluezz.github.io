DailyQuests: RPG Habits

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026 · Effective: 2 May 2026

Short version. DailyQuests runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no advertising, no analytics, no third-party trackers, and no outbound network requests.

1. Who we are

DailyQuests: RPG Habits (the “App”) is developed and published by Moozy Games (“we”, “us”). For privacy questions, contact us at moozygames@proton.me.

2. Data we collect

None. The App does not collect personal data. Specifically, the App does not collect, transmit, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  • Account credentials — the App has no accounts and no sign-in.
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or persistent tracking IDs.
  • Location data, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera input.
  • Crash logs, telemetry, performance metrics, or analytics events.
  • Behavioural data, in-app event streams, or usage profiles.

3. Data stored on your device

The App stores its state locally on your device so that your progress persists between sessions. This includes the quests you create, your character progress, settings, and similar gameplay data. This data never leaves your device. You can delete all of it at any time by clearing the App’s data or uninstalling the App.

4. Network & third parties

The App makes no outbound network requests. There are no embedded analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, no crash reporting services, no remote configuration, and no third-party content loaded at runtime. Operating-system-level traffic (for example, OS update checks or App Store communication) is handled by your device and is outside the App’s control.

5. Advertising

The App does not display advertisements and does not integrate with any ad network or measurement SDK.

6. Backups

Automatic system backups of the App’s data are disabled (Android allowBackup=false). Your gameplay data is not copied to cloud backup services by the App.

7. Permissions

The App requests only the minimum permissions required for its features. The App does not request the SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM permission. The App does not access contacts, location, the microphone, or the camera.

8. Children

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected at all, the App is compliant with children’s privacy frameworks such as COPPA and the GDPR-K provisions by design.

9. Purchases

Monetization is disabled in this version of the App. There are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no payment processing performed by the App.

10. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to access, export, correct, or delete on your behalf. You retain full control of the data stored locally on your device and can remove it at any time.

11. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how the App handles data — for example, by introducing an optional sync feature — we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after such a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
